About Me

Short Story About Me

Hello Everyone, Let me introduce myself. My name is Giri Diwa Adam, I am a Fullstack Web developer who comes from Tangerang City, Banten, precisely in the village of North Larangan. The beginning I liked the world of programming when I was in vocational school when I accidentally found a book about programming, thats where I started to become interested in the world of programming.

And finally I continued to study at the IT Department, and I became more and more interested in exploring the world of programming. To continue my curiosity about the world of programming, I took programming language lessons to deepen the programming language that I was studying.

Through this portfolio, I want to show the results of the projects I worked on as a fullstack programmer, hopefully it can inspire friends who want to become programmers too. and dont give up, keep learning until you can, because there is no waiting in the world of programming, they will continue to develop with the times.
